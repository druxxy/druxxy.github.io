Ivan Reis

Ivan Reis is a Brazilian comics artist. He is known for his work on comic books such as Dark Horse Comics' Ghost, Marvel Comics' Captain Marvel and Avengers Icons: The Vision and DC Comics' Action Comics, Green Lantern and Aquaman series. Other work for Dark Horse included The Mask, Time Cop, and Xena. He later worked for Lightning Comics.
